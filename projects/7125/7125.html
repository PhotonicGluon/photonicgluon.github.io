7125 is an EP exploring themes of secrecy, identity, and revelations.

This is my first 'real' attempt at making my own compositions in MuseScore, a free and open-source notation application. Created over 4 months, I am proud of how the five tracks turned out. They are experimental pieces as I try out new music techniques and notation styles for the first time.
