About Me

Hello! Welcome to my site! I hope you like it!

I am Kan Onn Kit (Ryan Kan), a Singaporean. I am known as PhotonicGluon online. I am a programmer by trade and a hobbyist writer and music composer on the side.

I studied at Nanyang Junior College (NYJC). My education there allowed me to foster teamwork and collaboration skills, as well as develop a good work ethic. I am often described as a level-headed person when making decisions. I consider the facts and evidence of the matter instead of relying (solely) on emotion and feeling. I am sociable and love to work in a team. Challenges are appealing to me, as they allow me to showcase my problem-solving skillset and come up with a solution.
